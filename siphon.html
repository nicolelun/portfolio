Siphon

Publication concept that offers readers a discovery-based reading experience. Each issue is dedicated to an everyday thing. Readers explore facts, definitions, quotes, and anecdotes to discover the history and significance of the everyday thing, which itself goes unnamed until the end of the issue.

Examples: “Legend has it that Ethiopian shephards discovered _____ over 2,000 years ago when they noticed their goats ‘dancing’ with energy after eating wild _____ cherries.” “_____ trees can grow up to 30 feet tall. Today, they are cultivated to about 10 feet tall for easier harvesting.” “‘_____ is a way of stealing time that should by rights belong to your older self’ - Terry Pratchett, Thud!”. (What is this issue about? Coffee.)
