Triptych

Prints inspired by the North Lawndale neighborhood on the West Side of Chicago. A neighborhood of only 3.2 square miles, Lawndale has over 100 churches, many located in converted storefronts, residences, synagogues, and other types of buildings. In the decades since the mid-20th century, the neighbhorhood has survived against the crippling legacies of racial discrimination, economic disinvestment, population loss, failing schools, and crime.
